Toasted bagel
    Time: 5 minutes
    Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bagel 1
butter 1 Tablespoon

To start, cut your bagel and toast it on low heat until browned. Apply the butter and spread evenly.

Grilled Cheese
    Time: 10 minutes
    Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2
Butter 1 Tablespoon
cheese slice 1

Start by putting your butter into a pan. Melt it and add your bread. Heat until bread browned. Add cheese to one bread slice and add the other bread onto it. Heat until cheese is melted.

Spaghetti
    Time: 20 minutes
    Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Noodles 1 pack
Butter 1 Tablespoon
spaghetti sauce 1 cup

Boil some water in a pot. Add your noodles and boil for 4 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in butter. Once served, add sauce as needed.
